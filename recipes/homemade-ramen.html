Homemade Ramen Recipe

Ramen is a dish that I have always wanted to make from scratch. I grew up on ramen so its only right to include it here.

You can do so much with it and add so much to this dish. If you don't like something, great remove it but this is what I like in mine. This recipe serves two people. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles (throw away the seasoning packets)
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable/miso broth
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp of ginger
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup of sliced mushrooms, (shiitake)
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Optional toppings: Sriracha, nori, sesame seeds, corn, tofu, etc.

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs
    • Bring the water to a boil, place the eggs in the water gentlely and then let them boil for 20 mins then put in cold water.
  2. Make the broth
    • Inside of a pot put a little oil inside over medium heat.
    • Saute garlic and ginger for 1 to 2 minutes.
    • Add soy sauce, sesame oil, miso paste (if you prefer to use), and chicken broth.
    • Bring it to a gentle boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Cook the Noodles
    • Add ramen noodles to the broth and cook for about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add Vegetables
    • Add in the mushrooms and greens in the last 1-2 minutes of cooking.
  5. Assemble
    • Peel and halve the boiled eggs.
    • Pour the ramen into bowls, top with the eggs, green onions, and any other toppings you would like
